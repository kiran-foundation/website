Kiran Foundation

Kiran Jain

17th Oct 1955 - 15th June 2015

Kiran Foundation is established in memory of Late Smt. Kiran Jain. She had endured lots of hardship in her life, but always kept smile and hope true to her name. With this foundation we are hoping that her life will become inspiration for other people to bring happiness and progress in their life.

Vision


Our vision is to enable promising and bright minds from underprivileged and marginalized section of society, can reach their full potential. Build a support system that can empower them with financial, emotional and mental support so that they can fulfil their dreams and reach where they deserve to be.

Programs


We currently work in two broad categories:
Underprivilage kids education: We are starting a scholarship, where we will support bright underprivilged kids. Our missition is to take support 1000 kids through this program by 2021.
Disability awareness: Make india friendlier towards disabled people.

Kiran Scholarship Program

Every year in india many bright young kids let their dreams go. They give up thier hope, dreams and potential to and are forced to spend their energies to battle harsh realities of life. Many of them born into poor or uneducated families. These kids have immense capabilities and they just need a push a guiding hand.

Our aim through this program is to identify exceptional but underprivileged kids and support them in their endeavours of Education, Sports or Art according to their interest and abilities.


Disabled Awareness

India has about 80 million disabled people. They are never a factor in infrstructure, transportation etc. Forget govt. even many big real estate developer build close their eys towards disabled while building apartment complexes. On top of that he attitude of society and prejudice makes India one of the worst contries for disabled.

Disabled don't need money, they need support system so that they can live independently. We are looking to do some awareness campaign as well as some long term programs. If you have any suggestions, or would like to participate please reach out to us.

Kiran Scholarship - 2016

Kiran Foundation will offer scholarship and support to two exceptional but underprivileged kids in their endeavours of Education, Sports or Art according to their interest and abilities. Foundation will be offering active guidance and personalised mentoring along with Financial Support. The sole aim of foundation will be to make this kids capable and successful.

Financial Support

The foundation will provide complete financial support to aid the child towards their persuite. This includes fee, travel, puchase of books, equipments etc.


Personal Mentoring

Our team at foundation will work closely with each child. The team will gather nacessary resources and connections. Each child will be provided a personal mentor, who will be available for support at any given time.


Eligibility

  1. Monthly income of the Family should be less than Rs. 10,000.
  2. Child should have passed 8th Standard or 14 Years of age.
  3. Child should have shown enormous potential in his/her field of interest.


Important Dates

Last date for submitting the application is 31st March 2016.

Children shortlisted for personal discussion will be intimated by phone before 30th May 2016


Apply

Please send the application along with following detail:

  1. Name, Address, Phone number etc.
  2. Name of mother and father and means of living
  3. Date of birth, qualification of the kid
  4. Why should this scholarship be given to the kid.

Please send the application along with following detail:

  1. Attested photocopies of Last three years mark sheets.
  2. Attested photocopies of certificates of achievements if any
  3. Recommendation letter from two independent people (along with the phone number, email, address or the person Recommending)
