Why learn Digital Skills

Kiran foundation presents "Essential Digital Skills", Digital skills are essential in the modern age. Women should learn them to navigate technology, protect against fraud, and access various practical tools like Canva, website building, social media, and mobile skills. These skills empower women to excel in the digital realm, promote online safety, and leverage technology for personal and professional growth. By equipping themselves with digital skills, women can thrive in the digital landscape and harness its vast potential for personal and professional advancement.

Why learn Financial Skills

Kiran foundation presents "Essential Finance Skills For Women", this is a special course designed with essential information about money and finance, which every women of this nation should be aware of. This course covers topic such as Banking System, Basic Behavorial Economics, Savings, Overview of various Investment Options, Investing in Gold, Importance of Insurance, Preparing a useful will etc. The course will last 8 weeks from 21st May 2022 to 9th July 2022. Classes will be every Saturday at 3:30 PM IST. We will try to answer all your queries during the course. The participants who complete all classes will also receive a certifacte at the end of the course.

Why learn Law for Woman

Kiran foundation presents "Everyday Law For Women", this is a special course designed with essential legal information, which every women of this nation should be aware of. This course covers topic such as sexual harassment at workplace, right to private defense, domestic violence, right to free legal aid, maternity benefits, right to get equal share in the inheritance and about national commision for women. The course will last three weeks, with two classes every week on Wednesday and Friday at 2:00 PM. We will try to answer all your queries during the course. The participants who complete all 6 classes will also receive a certifacte at the end of the course.
