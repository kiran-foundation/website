    <div>
      We are thrilled to share the incredible results from the Avtaran Diwas Competitions, held to celebrate the birthday (October 17th) of our inspiration, Ma Kiran Jain! The immense talent showcased beautifully reflects the mission of Kiran Pratibha (education) and Kiran Shakti (women's empowerment). A huge congratulations to all our creative winners and dedicated participants.
    </div>
  - |
    <div style="padding-top: 24px;">
      This year's celebrations featured several exciting competitions. The AI-Generated Image Competition challenged participants to use advanced tools like Gemini, Bing, or Canva AI to visualize "The Future We Create through Kiran Foundation."
    </div>
  - |
    <div style="padding-top: 24px;">
      Additionally, we celebrated the festive season with two Diwali-themed events: the Wall Decoration Competition, where participants decked their spaces with beautiful and festive designs, and the Rangoli Competition, where the ground came alive with vibrant colors and intricate designs.
    </div>
  - |
    <div style="padding-top: 24px;">
      Kiran Foundation extends a heartfelt thank you to every student and participant who submitted their work and video reels, making this year's Avtaran Diwas celebration a grand success. Your passion and commitment truly elevated the event! Congratulations to all the winners on their well-deserved prizes.
    </div>
